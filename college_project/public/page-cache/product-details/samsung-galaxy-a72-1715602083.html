Most Highlighted Features of Samsung Galaxy A72

Super AMOLED display: The Galaxy A72 features a 6.7-inch Super AMOLED Infinity-O Display with a Full HD+ resolution, offering vibrant colors and deep contrasts. The screen supports a 90Hz refresh rate, making every interaction smooth and responsive. Whether youre watching movies or scrolling through social media, the display brings content to life with stunning clarity.

 Quad-camera setup: With a quad-camera setup that includes a 64MP main sensor, a 12MP ultra-wide lens, an 8MP telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom, and a 5MP macro lens. This versatile system allows for high-resolution photos, wide-angle shots, close-up details, and zoomed-in pictures without losing quality. The camera also supports 4K video recording and OIS (Optical Image Stabilization), ensuring your memories are captured with the utmost precision.

 5,000mAh Long-Lasting Battery: The device is powered by a 5,000mAh battery, which is more than enough to get you through a full day of heavy usage. It also supports 25W fast charging, so you can quickly power up and get back to what matters most. With this kind of battery life, you can stream, game, and work without constantly worrying about your next charge.

Snapdragon 720G chipset: Under the hood, the Galaxy A72 runs on a Snapdragon 720G chipset paired with 8GB of RAM. This combination ensures smooth performance, whether youre multitasking between apps or playing graphics-intensive games. The phone also comes with One UI 3.1, based on Android 11, providing a clean and intuitive user interface that enhances the overall user experience.

Samsung Galaxy A72 Price in Bangladesh 

Galaxy A72s official price is BDT 53,999 for the 8/256 GB variant.  The Phone is also available in 3 different variants (6GB RAM + 128GB Storage), (8GB RAM + 128GB Storage), and (6GB RAM + 256GB Storage). Its available in 4 different colors: Awesome Black, Awesome White, Awesome Violet, and Awesome Blue.

Why Should You Buy Samsung Galaxy A72?

It is an excellent choice for those seeking a smartphone that balances performance with value. Its 6.7-inch Super AMOLED display with a 90Hz refresh rate ensures a visually stunning experience, while the 64MP quad-camera system offers versatility for all your photography needs. The 5,000mAh battery provides all-day endurance, and the Snapdragon 720G chipset with 8GB of RAM promises smooth multitasking and gaming. With these features, the A72 stands out as a device that delivers a premium experience without the premium price, making it a smart purchase for savvy consumers.

Final Verdict

This smartphone is a testament to Samsungs commitment to quality and innovation. Its a device that offers a premium experience without the premium price tag, making it an excellent choice for consumers looking for value and performance in one package
